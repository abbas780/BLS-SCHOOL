BLS EDUCATION

Shri G.P. Sahu.

Bajrangi Lal Sahu Inter College is a co-educational institution from the kindergarten to class XII Founded by Shri G.P. Sahu. The school is affiliated to the U.P Board Allahabad and the students appear in the U.P Board Examinations of 10+2 pattern conducted by the Board. Founded in 1975, Bajrangi Lal Sahu Inter College has a long standing in the field of education. At Bajrangi Lal Sahu Inter College Education is more than text books, examinations and marks. It is the basic foundation on which the men and women of tomorrow prepare to meet the challenges of a complex and ever-changing world.

Known for his commitment to education and the well-being of his students, Mr. Sahu brings a visionary approach to the institution. Under his leadership, BLS Inter College has seen remarkable progress in academics, discipline, and overall development. Mr Sandeem. Pre-primary

The Pre-primary years in BLS inculcate in children the qualities of curiosity, exploration and discovery in a fun-filled, safe and happy learning environment. The emphasis is on developing in students love of learning, social skills, moral values, and self-confidence. Teachers ensure this by letting the students explore, know, understand and form their own ideas.

Primary

BLS aims to provide a joyful learning environment that empowers primary aged children and develops their self-confidence and competency in the core subjects. BLS seeks to create a stimulating learning environment that both supports and challenges the individual child. As they grow, children develop the qualities of hard work, good manners and self-discipline. Most importantly, they come out as self assured and confident. .

Juniors

Students of the 11-13 age group are emerging as junior youth and curious young adolescents. They need plenty of academic stimulation and intellectually challenging material. The foundations of solid learning laid here set the stage for academic success in the senior years.

Seniors

BLS sets extremely high standards of academic scholarship. There is an emphasis on learning by doing, especially in science and computing. Students are provided with strategies and the keys to success in Board Examinations that help them to plan better and study smarter, not just harder. A strong remedial teaching program helps weaker students to become secure in their knowledge.

PHILOSOPHY

At BLS, we believe that education for the 21st Century must be both broader and bolder . A modern school needs to do much more than ensuring good marks on a sheet, which is the minimum responsibility of a school. A ‘good’ education must produce much more: a ‘good’ human being with a broad range of capabilities.

A BLS education takes into account the three realities of a human being: material,human , and divine. In turn, CMS education is based on four building blocks, developing in children Universal Values, Global Understanding , a spirit of Service to the World , and an attitude of Excellence in All Things .
